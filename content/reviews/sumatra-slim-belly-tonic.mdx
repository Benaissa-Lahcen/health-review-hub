## Key Ingredients Breakdown

### Valerian Root
A well-known herb used for centuries to promote relaxation and quality sleep. Poor sleep is strongly linked to weight gain and metabolic issues.

### Humulus Lupulus (Hops)
Beyond its use in brewing, hops extract may help with metabolism and stress reduction. Some studies suggest it can support healthy inflammatory responses.

### Griffonia Simplicifolia (5-HTP)
This plant extract contains 5-HTP, a precursor to serotonin. It may help with mood, appetite control, and sleep quality.

### Berberine
One of the most well-researched natural compounds for metabolic health. Studies show it may support healthy blood sugar levels and metabolism.

### Spirulina Blue
A nutrient-dense blue-green algae packed with antioxidants, vitamins, and minerals. It provides nutritional support while you manage your weight.

### Inulin
A prebiotic fiber that feeds beneficial gut bacteria. A healthy gut microbiome is increasingly linked to easier weight management.
