## Why Proper Support Matters

The lumbar region (lower back) bears the most weight and stress when you sit. Without proper support, the natural inward curve of your spine flattens, leading to muscle strain, pinched nerves, and chronic pain.

**The Solution:** Using a specialized spinal support cushion acts as a bridge, filling the gap between your back and the chair, ensuring your spine stays in a neutral, healthy alignment.
